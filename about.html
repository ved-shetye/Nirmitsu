Our Nirmitsu Story

Pioneering sustainable manufacturing through innovative Additive Manufacturing Technology

Nirmitsu Design Labs Private Limited was founded in August 2024 with the sole premise of manufacturing in plastic and composite custom parts – and being environmentally friendly at the same time.

Firmly believing Additive Manufacturing, aka 3D Printing, would make this happen and have a beneficial impact on production/manufacturing in each and every sector.

It is a Start Up registered with the Central Government having the Startup India Certificate of Recognition.

Goa, being one of the greenest state in the country, has always aimed to have non polluting industries while sufficing the need of the its people and the businesses. Nirmitsu Design Labs will be the pioneer in the state of Goa offering these sustainable manufacturing services and contribute to the economy of the State, the Nation as well as of the World.

Our Vision

Our vision is to lead the transformation of industries with pioneering additive manufacturing technology and global quality standards.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower innovation by delivering advanced manufacturing solutions that redefine quality and precision in the digital age.
